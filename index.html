What is a Text Cleaner?

A text cleaner is an online tool designed to clean and format text by removing unwanted characters and performing complex text operations. Its primary purpose is to unformat text and eliminate unmeaningful characters, especially from content copied from various sources. This process is crucial for ensuring that the text is clear, professional, and free of distractions.

Text cleaners handle a variety of formatting issues. They can remove extra spaces, special characters, line breaks, and other unwanted elements. The tool can also change letter case, delete duplicate lines, strip HTML tags, and convert URLs into clickable links, making text easier to work with.
