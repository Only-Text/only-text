How to Use the Only Text Tool Effectively

January 10, 2025 · Only Text Team

The Only Text tool is very straightforward to use, but here are some tips to ensure you get the most out of it. Whether you want to quickly sanitize text for a plain-text application or prepare text for further processing, these steps will guide you through the process.

Steps to Clean Your Text:

  1. Open the Only Text tool: Navigate to the Only Text homepage where the tool is available (no download or install required).
  2. Paste your text: In the "Enter text" box, paste or type the text that you want to clean up.
  3. Set the options: By default, the tool will remove emojis and punctuation. You can uncheck "Remove Emojis" or "Remove Punctuation" if you want to keep those in your text.
  4. Get the cleaned text: As you type or paste, the "Cleaned text" box will show the result in real-time. You’ll also see a count of characters and words update.
  5. Copy or download: Use the "Copy" button to copy the cleaned text to your clipboard, or hit "Download" to save it as a text file.

That’s it! Following these steps, you can quickly convert any snippet of text to plain text. The live preview and counters help ensure you get exactly what you need. With Only Text, text cleanup is just a matter of seconds.
