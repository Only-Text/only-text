Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

TL;DR: Only Text processes all your text locally in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit your text data to any server. Your privacy is our top priority.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Only Text. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our text cleaning tool at onlytext.app (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Data Processing

2.1 Client-Side Processing

All text processing happens entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your text never leaves your device
  • We do not have access to the content you process
  • No text data is transmitted to our servers
  • No text data is stored on our servers

2.2 Local Storage

We do not use cookies or local storage to save your text. When you close your browser or refresh the page, your text is cleared from memory.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our Service. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referral source (how you found our site)

Important: Google Analytics does NOT collect or have access to the text you process in our tool.

3.2 Technical Data

Our web server automatically collects standard technical information:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser user agent
  • Request timestamps
  • HTTP status codes

This data is used solely for security, performance monitoring, and debugging purposes.

4. How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Improve the Service and user experience
  • Understand usage patterns and popular features
  • Detect and prevent technical issues
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics for website analytics. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5.2 Font Awesome & Google Fonts

We use Font Awesome and Google Fonts for icons and typography. These services may collect technical data when loading resources. See their respective privacy policies for details.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our Service (e.g., hosting providers)

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Minimal data collection principle
  • Client-side processing to eliminate data transmission risks

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking in your browser
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data (though we collect minimal data)

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. International Users

Our Service is hosted in the European Union. If you access our Service from outside the EU, your data may be transferred to and processed in the EU. By using our Service, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our homepage. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@onlytext.app

Remember: Your text is processed entirely in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit the content you clean with Only Text. Your privacy is guaranteed by design.
